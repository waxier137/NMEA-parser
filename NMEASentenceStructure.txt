NMEA 0183 Message format
$ <Address> {,value} *<checksum> <CR><LF>

Address field: <Address>
     - Only digits and uppercase letters, cannot be null
     - Divided into 2 fields: <XX><XXX>
     - <XX> : Talker identifier: always GP for a GPS receiver, P For proprietary Messages
     - <XXX>: Sentence Formatter: Define message content

Data fields: {,value}
     - Delimited by a ","
     - Length can vary for a certain field

Checksum field: *<checksum>
     - Starts with a "*"
     - Consists of 2 characters representing a hex number
     - Checksum = Exclusive OR of all characters between "$" and "*"

End sequence: <CR><LF>
     - Always <CR><LF>
